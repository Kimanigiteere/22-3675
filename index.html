Maintenance Guide

Complete Car Maintenance Checklist

  • Engine Care
    • Oil change every 5,000 km
    • Air filter replacement
    • Spark plug inspection
  • Tyres & Brakes
    • Check tyre pressure monthly
    • Rotate tyres every 10,000 km
    • Inspect brake pads for wear
  • Fluid Levels
    • Coolant top-up
    • Brake fluid check
    • Power steering fluid
  • Electrical System
    • Battery terminal cleaning
    • Check all lights and indicators
  • Exterior & Body
    • Wash and wax every 2 weeks
    • Inspect for rust or paint chips
    • Clean and condition rubber seals
  • Interior Upkeep
    • Vacuum and wipe down surfaces
    • Condition leather seats

Proper maintenance is the backbone of any high-performance vehicle. Neglecting routine checks can reduce engine life by up to 40%, increase fuel consumption, and void manufacturer warranties.

For supercar owners, factory-recommended service intervals are especially critical. Many exotic engines operate under extreme tolerances — even a slightly degraded oil film can cause catastrophic bearing failure at high RPM.
